Welcome!         :)
We introcude the EPFL Telegram Bot. This bot will help you during your stay at EPFL.
In fact, it will make your student life easier by integrating various EPFL campus services into your daily social interactions.
Also, by enabling collaborative use of existing or new campus services, the EPFL bot brings students together and promotes a more sociable and fun campus experience.


Please sign up by providing your EPFL email address below so that we add you to our weekly EPFLBot newsletter.
