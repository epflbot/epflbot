EPFL Telegram Bot

Welcome! :)
We introduce the EPFL Telegram Bot. This bot will help you during your stay at EPFL.
It will make your student life easier by integrating various EPFL campus services into your daily social interactions.
Also, by enabling collaborative use of EPFL's campus services, the bot brings students together and promotes a more sociable and fun campus experience.

Feedback is Always Appreciated!

The EPFL Bot project is still relatively new. So, please bear with us :)
We truly appreciate and encourage every feedback. So, if you have any improvement you would like to suggest, or any useful idea/feature that you think should be added to the bot in order to further cater to your needs, please leave us your feedback using the Telegram command "/feedback (message)"!
